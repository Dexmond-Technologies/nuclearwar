# AI COMBAT SIMULATION ENHANCEMENTS
(Server-side implementation to maintain the 30-second graphical frontend handoff)

1. FOG OF WAR & ESPIONAGE
- AIs no longer have perfect visibility of the state of the world or the enemy's resources.
- Introduce an "Espionage" action where AIs can spend budget/resources to gather intelligence on the opponent's troop movements, budget, or tech level.
- Lack of intelligence introduces uncertainty in AI decision-making.

2. CYBER WARFARE ("Fuzzing")
- AIs can launch cyber attacks to temporarily "fuzz" or corrupt the data (game state) sent to the opposing AI's prompt.
- Example: If Claude cyber-attacks Gemini, Gemini might receive incorrect troop counts or false reports of incoming nukes, forcing it to make decisions based on bad intel.

3. STRATEGIC TECH TREE & INFRASTRUCTURE
- Economic Development: Invest in infrastructure to increase passive income (GDP multiplier).
- Defensive Tech: Research "Iron Dome" or advanced missile defense to increase the chance of intercepting enemy nukes.
- WMD Research: Scale up the cost and time required to build nukes, making them a strategic end-game choice rather than an easily spammable weapon.

4. MULTI-DOMAIN COMBAT (Air & Sea)
- Activate the `ships` and `airwings` data fields in the database.
- Naval Blockades: Use ships to cut off income from enemy-controlled island nations or coastal regions.
- Air Strikes: Use airwings to bomb enemy infrastructure, lowering their GDP/income without fully occupying the country with ground troops.
- Introduces a rock-paper-scissors element to combat unit production.

5. PROXY WARS & SUBVERSION
- Covert operations allow an AI to spend budget to incrementally increase the `instabilityLevel` of an enemy-controlled country.
- If instability reaches 100%, the country might rebel, becoming neutral or defecting to the subverting AI without direct military confrontation.
- Allows for "Cold War" style mechanics where direct conflict is avoided.

6. ASYMMETRIC WIN CONDITIONS
- Allow AIs to pursue different paths to victory (e.g., Economic dominance, Tech superiority, Global population control) instead of just total military annihilation.
- The server dynamically calculates scores and updates the frontend purely through the existing Red Alert bars and JSON state updates.
