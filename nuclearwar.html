Game Overview

What It Is

Nuclear War is a Risk-inspired global domination game rendered on a realistic 3D Earth globe. Every country in the world (~180 nations) is a playable territory with its own troop count, faction color, and vulnerability to nuclear strikes. The game uses NASA Earth satellite imagery as the globe texture, with Natural Earth GeoJSON borders overlaid on top.

Game Modes

  • Single Player: You (Blue) vs 3 AI opponents (Red, Green, Gold). Dynamic AI uses greedy territorial expansion and opportunistic nuclear strikes.
  • Multiplayer: Online play via WebSocket server with 4-letter room codes. Up to 4 human players; any unfilled slots are controlled by AI. Includes in-game text chat.

Phases Per Turn

Reinforce
Place new troops on your territories. You receive a minimum of 3 troops, plus 1 additional troop per 3 territories you own.
Attack
Click your territory, then click an enemy territory to initiate an attack. Conventional attacks are resolved by live dice rolls determining the outcome.
Fortify
Move troops strategically between your own connected territories to bolster your defenses.
Nuke
A devastating special attack that ignores dice rolls. Deals 6-10 massive damage and leaves a 3-turn radioactive fallout. Each player starts with 2 nuclear warheads.
End Turn
Pass the command to the next player. Survive and conquer.

UNITED FRONT: DEFEATING RAINCLOUD

RAINCLOUD is an evolving AI adversary that continuously adapts, hacks global infrastructure, and launches devastating nuclear strikes. As individual commanders, you will fall. Humanity can only survive by fighting as a single, united coalition.

⚡ The Global Economy

All human players generate and share a single Defense Budget every turn. This unified economy is vital for survival, but frequent global catastrophes—such as pandemics, market crashes, or power grid failures—will drastically drain your funds. You must collectively decide when to save and when to spend.

⚔ Strategic Command Perks

Commanders must pool their resources to execute high-tier operations via the Command Uplink. These perks are essential to break the AI's defenses. Example operations include:

  • Satellite Hack: Blinds RAINCLOUD's orbital reconnaissance, reducing its intelligence.
  • Cyberattack: Deploys a zero-day exploit to drain RAINCLOUD's energy to 0.
  • Airborne Assault: Drops paratroopers directly behind enemy lines to secure territories instantly.

🛡 Cooperative Synergy

As RAINCLOUD's Threat Tier escalates, individual conventional attacks will end in slaughter. Cooperative synergy is the only way to win. Combine your operations: have one commander execute an EMP Blast to strip enemy troops and apply 'Shock' effects, while another commander moves in with conventional infantry to secure the weakened territory.

How to Play Together

Use the built-in Command Chat to coordinate your turns. Call out your targets, agree on which perks to deploy to avoid wasting budget, and protect each other's borders. Remember that the AI analyzes global troop distributions and will ruthlessly exploit any weak points in the human frontline.

Technical Stack

Frontend: HTML5 + CSS3 + Vanilla JavaScript
3D Engine: Three.js r128
Backend: Node.js + WebSockets
